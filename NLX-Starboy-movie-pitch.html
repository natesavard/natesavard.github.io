NLX STARBOY — Movie Version / Pitch

This is the visual pitch version of the comic universe. A cinematic read for studios, comic people, and anyone who needs to understand how this world looks, feels, and moves on screen.

The Vision

We’re NOT making:

generic comic

cheesy sci-fi

We ARE making:

Cinematic visual drops

Think:

dark techno film

fashion campaign energy

surreal + real blended

almost like memories leaking through reality

Format

Instagram / TikTok / Shorts Series

Each episode = 1 core scene

10–30 seconds

minimal dialogue

strong visuals

text overlays (clean, not spammy)

Visual:

packed rave

slow motion crowd

sweat / lights / gold tones

Gemstone at decks (calm, locked in)

“every city has a frequency…”

Episode 2 — The Watcher

Visual:

crowd moving

eyes locked on Gemstone

“not dancing… just watching”

Episode 3 — The Freestyle

Visual:

energy building

Then:

micro glitch (VERY subtle)

Episode 4 — The Fracture

Visual:

someone yelling:

“GEM!”

everyone looks up

sky splits (thin line at first)

light breaks through

Then:

deep bass hit → white flash

Episode 5 — The Desert

Visual:

silence

Gemstone waking up in sand

wind

nothing else

Then:

massive ship reveal above

Episode 6 — The Oracle

Visual:

interior glowing environment
